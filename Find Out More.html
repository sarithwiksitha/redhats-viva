Campus Facilities

At SIT, we have over 50 Lecture Halls with a capacity of over 200 seats. Together with comepetent Lectureres and state of the art laborataries that can be used to do practical experiments, SIT can provide you an education that is upto international standards and more...

Interested in Dramas? Musicals? At the Theater, there are various dramas and shows being performed at least once a week by the enthusiastic students of the drama and music clubs. Love Dancing? There are dance performances and shows being held every month or so, and on special occassions, you can also invite famous musicians and hold shows in the theater with the supervision of the senior students. So have a great time, and enjoy the show!

The Library...the repository of knowledge. Ranging from fiction to non-fictional books, to auto-biographies of great men and women of the past, and to books about various subjects such as Computer Technoligy, Agriculture, Mathematics, Engineering, etc. Students have a few restrictions when entering the Library such as that they cannot enter with their bags. A seperate compartment is provided to keep you bags when entering the library. Keep you Student ID cards with you at all times when entering and exiting the library. And always, ALWAYS, BE QUIET and do not disturb the others when in the library.

The Students' Study Room was built with the sole purpose of serving as a quiet place for the students to study and do their projects. Here, Students can relax and do their lecture studies leisurely, have club meetings or have project discussions without disturbances.

Students can relax and hang out in the students' Lounge area after the lectures. Play games with your friends or play a video game with your phone or computer while waiting for the next lecture. Or read a book- whatever helps you realx and enjoy a quick break.

The Students' Dormitory is on a seprate building within the campus. Female Dormitories are within the campus while Male Dormitories are outside the campus. But without distinction both dormitories possess state of the art facilities and a tight security that makes living in the dormotories both a safe and a comfortable experience. Students can request single rooms, but most of the time , they are provided double rooms. The Students can pay at the start of the semester, with Rs.50,000 for a semester.

With state of the art facilities and equipments, students can now do various practicals and projects in the computer labs within the campus. Computer labaratories are opened from 9.00 am and closes at 11.00pm. During lab sessions, students can ask questions from the lab instructors and get their advice for various projects.
